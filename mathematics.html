Project Arch

"Discover your Passion"

Project Arch is our solution for the discontent towards the current education system. Instead of the current system that’s both outdated and boring, students are now able to freely pick and choose what they want to learn, from a wide range of dynamic, project- and practical-based tutorials.

Made by students, for students.
